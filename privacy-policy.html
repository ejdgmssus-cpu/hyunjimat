Privacy Policy

현지맛 (HyunjiMat) · Last updated: March 31, 2026

1. Introduction

HyunjiMat ("we", "our", "the app") is a local recommendation app that analyzes public YouTube video comments to help users discover restaurants, cafes, hospitals, and other local businesses in Korea. We are committed to protecting user privacy and handling data responsibly.

2. Information We Collect

Location Data: The app requests access to your device's location to provide location-based recommendations. This data is processed locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

Search Queries: When you search for a keyword (e.g., "파주 맛집"), the search query is sent to our server to retrieve results. We do not associate search queries with any personal identifiers.

Saved Places: Places you save are stored locally on your device using iOS UserDefaults. This data is not transmitted to any server.

3. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or process:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Account credentials or login information
  • Payment or financial information
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Browsing history or app usage analytics
  • Photos, contacts, or other personal data

4. YouTube API Services

Our app uses the YouTube API Services to search for videos and retrieve publicly available comments. By using our app, you also agree to be bound by the YouTube Terms of Service and Google Privacy Policy.

We access only publicly available data through the YouTube API:

  • Public video search results (video titles, channel names)
  • Public comments and their like counts
  • Comment author display names (publicly visible on YouTube)

We do not access any private YouTube user data, and we do not require YouTube account authentication from our users.

5. Data Storage and Caching

Search results are temporarily cached on our server for up to 24 hours to improve performance and reduce API usage. Cached data contains only aggregated place recommendations derived from public YouTube comments and does not include any personal user information.

6. Third-Party Services

Our app integrates with the following third-party services:

  • YouTube Data API v3: To search videos and retrieve public comments
  • Google Gemini AI: To analyze comments and extract place names (server-side only)
  • Naver Search API: To retrieve business information (address, category)
  • Naver Maps: To display place locations (via deep link)
  • Supabase: For server infrastructure and data caching

7. Data Security

All API keys are stored securely on our server and are never exposed in the app. Communication between the app and our server uses HTTPS encryption.

8. Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app displays publicly available YouTube comment data and does not target children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
